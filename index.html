Why Milwaukee

Milwaukee, The Brew City

Milwaukee, Wisconsin, is a vibrant city that offers a unique blend of rich history, diverse culture, and stunning lakefront views. Known for its thriving craft beer scene, the city is home to iconic breweries and festivals, making it a haven for beer enthusiasts.

Visitors can explore the fascinating museums like the Milwaukee Art Museum, attend a variety of festivals or immerse themselves in the city's industrial past at the Harley-Davidson Museum. With beautiful parks, the historic Third Ward district, and a growing food scene, Milwaukee combines Midwestern charm with modern attractions, making it a perfect destination for anyone seeking adventure, culture, and relaxation.

Festivals

Milwaukee is well known for it year-round festivals and activies. Some of the largest festivals include Summerfest, Brady Street Festival, Milwaukee Boat Show, Milwaukee Film Festival, Wisconsin State Fair, Bastille Days, Milwaukee Art Festival and many more.

Personal Favorite: Summerfest

Known as "The World's Largest Music Festival," Summerfest features hundreds of performances from national and international artists across various genres. The festival takes place on the beautiful lakefront at Henry Maier Festival Park and lasts 11 days.

Sports

Milwaukee, Wisconsin, has a strong sports culture, with passionate fans supporting a variety of teams across multiple sports. Here are some of the most popular sports teams in Milwaukee:

- Milwaukee Bucks (NBA): Basketball

- Milwaukee Brewers (MLB): Baseball

- Milwaukee Admirals (NHL): Hocky

- Milwaukee Wave (MASL): Futbol

Personal Favorite: Milwaukee Bucks

On game days you can find many fans flocking to the stadium, whether they have ticket to the game or if they plan to watch the game outside in the "deer district".

Food and Drink

Milwaukee is known for its hearty, flavorful foods like fish fry, bratwurst, and cheese curds, as well as its rich beverage culture, especially beer and the classic Old-Fashioned cocktail. The city's culinary scene reflects its German heritage, love for comfort food, and thriving craft beer industry, making Milwaukee a great destination for food lovers!

Personal Favorite: Cheese curds

Cheese curds are a Wisconsin classic, often found at local restaurants, bars, and food festivals. They are a must-try for anyone visiting the state.
